思君不见下渝州 | THINKING OF YOU GONE DOWN TO YUZHOU NOT SEEING YOU

胶合板、电视、数字打印，150x100x110cmx6，2025
Plywood, televisions, digital prints, 150 × 100 × 110 cm × 6, 2025
艺术家从网上下载了几首描写古代重庆（渝州、巴州）诗歌，并在如今繁华的重庆都市街巷与角落中寻找诗歌中所出现的汉字，最后将这些汉字重组为当年那些诗歌。
The artist downloaded several ancient poems describing Chongqing (historically known as Yuzhou or Bazhou) from the internet. Then, wandering through the bustling streets and alleys of contemporary Chongqing, the artist searched for the Chinese characters that appeared in these poems—found on shop signs, advertisements, newspapers, and public signage. Finally, these scattered characters were reassembled to recreate the original poems, bridging the past and present through the everyday urban landscape.
