图像帝国计划 | empire image project

This ongoing project is trying to redefine the relationship between visual and audience by using paintings and videos.

展览现场 ｜ SITE OF EXHIBITION

中国当代艺术新学院方式：北京电影学院新媒体艺术实验室特展，金鸡湖美术馆，苏州，2022
The New academic approach of Chinese Contemporary Art: Beijing Film Academy New Media Art Laboratory Exhibition, Jinji Lake Museum, Suzhou, 2022
《图像帝国》是一组持续了近十年的艺术计划，作品以看似幽默的方式，将电影场景中出现过的绘画复制出来，并再次与这个电影片段并置展出，一方面营造出具有穿透性空间关系，也是对于电影叙事一种抽离，是艺术家在哲学层面对图像时代的做出的智性回应。
Empire image project is an art projects that lasted nearly a decade. In a seemingly humorous way, the works reproduce the paintings that appeared in the film scenes and are displayed again juxtaposed with the film clips. On the one hand, the work creates a multi-level spatial relationship between the space on the spot and the space in the movie. On the other hand, it interrupts the audience from the process of watching the movie to avoid being captured by the Hollywood film industry. The artist's use of this work is a reflection of the image age we are living in.
